Police seek information on auto theft from hotel (Manning, Johnathan) November 8, 2018 at 4:25 PM CST - Updated November 8 at 4:37 PM
LAKE CHARLES, LA (KPLC) -The Lake Charles Police Department is investigating a motor vehicle theft of a white 2012 Kia Optima that was parked in a local hotel parking lot.
Police have released two photos of the vehicle and the suspect.
Sgt. Jeff Keenum is asking the anyone who may know the suspect to contact Det. Larry Newingham at 491-1456, ext. 5323. (LCPD)
