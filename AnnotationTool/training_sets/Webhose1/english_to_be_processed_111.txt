A hit-and-run Wednesday afternoon in San Francisco’s South of Market neighborhood left a man suffering from life-threatening injuries, police said.
The driver, a man between 25 and 30, hit the victim around 3:15 p.m. near the intersection of Seventh and Natoma streets in San Francisco, police said.

The suspect then fled on foot and the victim, 27, was transported to a hospital, authorities said.
