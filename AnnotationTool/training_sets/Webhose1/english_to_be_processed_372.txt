The District in Downtown Quincy is making holiday shopping easier for you this holiday season. On Thursdays between Thanksgiving and Christmas, several downtown businesses will be extending their hours even later to make it easier for you to get your shopping done. Thankful Thursdays will kick off on November 29 and run through December 20. And the best part? Santa will be at Washington Park each Thursday from 5:30 pm - 7:30 pm to meet with your little ones.
